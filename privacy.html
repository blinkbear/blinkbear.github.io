Privacy Policy for BlinkBear

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

Overview

BlinkBear ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed with privacy as a core principle.

Data Collection

BlinkBear does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

All app data remains on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third parties.

The app does not use analytics, cookies, tracking tools, or any third-party services (except Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases, which is managed entirely by Apple).

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your device only:

  • App Preferences: Your selected apps to monitor, monitoring settings, alert preferences (sound and haptic feedback), and theme customization choices
  • Streak Statistics: Your break completion statistics, current streak, longest streak, and unlocked milestone rewards
  • In-App Purchases: Records of premium features you've purchased (managed by Apple)

This data is stored using iOS standard storage methods (UserDefaults and local files) within your device's secure app sandbox and shared App Group container (to enable communication between the main app and system extensions).

Important: This local storage is standard iOS app functionality. The data is not identifiable, not transmitted anywhere, and only accessible by the app on your device.

Screen Time API Usage

BlinkBear uses Apple's Screen Time API (Family Controls framework) to:

  • Monitor usage of apps you select
  • Display break reminders based on the 20-20-20 rule (recommended by eye health professionals)
  • Temporarily block selected apps during break times to encourage healthy screen habits

Important: Screen Time data is processed entirely on your device by iOS. We do not have access to your Screen Time data beyond what you explicitly configure within the app. No usage data is collected, transmitted, or stored by us.

In-App Purchases

BlinkBear offers optional in-app purchases for premium themes and icons. These transactions are processed securely by Apple through the App Store.

We do not collect, access, or store your payment information. All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple's secure systems.

Your purchase history is managed by Apple and can be restored on your devices using your Apple ID.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

No third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools are integrated into this app.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security (encryption, passcode/biometric protection)
  • There is no risk of data breaches on our servers (we don't have servers)
  • Your privacy is protected by default

Children's Privacy

BlinkBear does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data remains on the device and no information is transmitted, the app is safe for users of all ages.

The app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) as no personal information is collected from users of any age.

Your Rights and Data Control

Data Access and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app (preferences, statistics, purchases)
  • Deletion: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app from your device
  • Backup: Your data is automatically backed up with your device backups (if you have iCloud or iTunes backup enabled)
  • No Server Data: No data exists on our servers, so there is nothing to request or delete from us

GDPR and CCPA Compliance

Since BlinkBear does not collect, process, or transmit personal information, GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) rights related to data access, portability, and deletion are not applicable.

However, we respect your privacy regardless of your location:

  • Right to information: This policy explains our practices (no collection)
  • Right to deletion: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Right to opt-out: Not applicable as no data is collected or sold

International Users

BlinkBear can be used worldwide. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no international data transfer concerns. All data processing occurs locally on your device, regardless of your location.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: blinkbear.app@gmail.com

We will respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.

Third-Party Services and Links

BlinkBear uses only the following Apple-provided services:

  • StoreKit: For in-app purchase processing (managed by Apple)
  • Family Controls Framework: For Screen Time API functionality (processed on-device by iOS)

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

If the app contains links to external websites or resources, those sites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites.

Apple's Privacy Practices

For information about how Apple handles data related to in-app purchases and the Screen Time API, please refer to:

  • Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  • App Store & Privacy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/features/
